### 0.0.1 / 2010-08-04

* Initial Release:
  * Provides:
    * Bar Graphs (Horizontal / Vertical)
    * Line Graphs
    * Dot Plots
    * Pie Charts
  * Provides `setColor`, `setOpacity`, `highlight`, `unhighlight`,
    `scale`, `raise` and `lower` actions on all graph elements.
  * Supports `click` and `hover` events on all graph elements.
